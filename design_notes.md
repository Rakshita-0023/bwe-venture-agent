# BWE Visual Design Notes

Reference reviewed: https://boldworldengineering.com/

Observed style signals to carry into the local dashboard:

- Background is almost black, closer to `#0a0a0a` than a soft dashboard charcoal.
- Primary text is bright off-white, with muted gray secondary copy and restrained border lines.
- Accent color is a sharp chartreuse / electric-lime, around `#ccff00`, used sparingly for emphasis.
- The site mood is bold, editorial, and slightly brutalist rather than glossy SaaS.
- Headings feel assertive and compressed in tone: large, high-contrast, minimal ornament, strong line breaks.
- Small labels use uppercase, tracking, and a more technical / system-like treatment.
- Cards are dark panels with subtle borders and modest rounding, not oversized soft floating tiles.
- Hover states feel crisp and intentional: small movement, accent edge, and contrast shifts instead of flashy animation.
- Navigation is minimal and clean, with the active state defined by contrast and accent rather than decorative gradients.
- The hero relies on typography, spacing, and attitude more than illustration-heavy chrome.

Dashboard implication:

- Keep the app dark, sharp, and branded.
- Use chartreuse accents only where they improve hierarchy.
- Prefer structured content panels, memo blocks, and intelligence cards over default dataframes.
- Make the ask experience feel like an internal strategy console that still belongs to the BWE world.
